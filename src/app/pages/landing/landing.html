The Problem

An informal economy stuck on phone calls and cash

Millions of South Africans in townships and rural communities are cut off from platforms like Uber, Uber Eats and Bolt Food — yet a thriving delivery economy already exists.

  • You need to know someone's phone number
  • Prices vary wildly with no transparency
  • No tracking, no ratings, no accountability
  • Cash only, and drivers struggle to find customers
Our Solution

One trusted platform for the whole community

Bossa Connect lets customers instantly request delivery or a ride from nearby registered providers — with transparent pricing, live tracking, secure payments and ratings that keep everyone accountable.
